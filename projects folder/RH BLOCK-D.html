Rashid Hospital Isolation Block-D

  • Category: Healthcare Facility
  • Client: Dubai Health Authority
  • Project Date: 2023
  • Location: Dubai, UAE
  • Project URL: www.dha.gov.ae

Project Description

Rashid Hospital Isolation Block-D is a specialized healthcare facility designed for infectious disease treatment and isolation care. The project involved comprehensive BIM coordination for advanced isolation systems, negative pressure environments, and specialized air filtration systems to ensure patient and staff safety.

Key responsibilities included clash detection for isolation equipment and utilities, coordination with infection control specialists, and ensuring compliance with international healthcare isolation standards and biosafety regulations.
