Project Description

Aqua Arabia is a premier water park and resort destination in Qiddiya, Saudi Arabia, featuring thrilling water rides, wave pools, and lazy rivers. Designed to provide fun for all ages, it combines exciting attractions with lush landscaping and modern amenities, making it a top choice for family entertainment and relaxation.
