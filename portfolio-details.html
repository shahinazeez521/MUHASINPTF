Rashid Hospital (Isolation Block-D)

In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, I contributed to the rapid development of a fully functional screening and isolation facility within just one week. This project was aimed at supporting the community by providing timely and dedicated care for Coronavirus patients. Despite the tight timeframe, we ensured that the facility was equipped with all essential medical services and infrastructure, without compromising on quality of care. The centre played a crucial role in managing the surge in cases, offering safe and efficient screening, isolation, and preliminary treatment for affected individuals.

NMC Specialty Hospital, Ghubra – Oman

In this project, I was responsible for the complete architectural design process, ensuring compliance with healthcare and Ministry of Health (MOH) guidelines while meeting client requirements. My role included preparing detailed architectural drawings such as ceiling layouts, flooring plans, reflected ceiling plans (RCP), curtain layouts, room layouts, and 3D visualizations. I coordinated with the line manager to obtain approvals and prepared comprehensive shop drawings, including specialized areas like CSSD, laboratories, dialysis units, and operating theaters (OT).
